RESOURCES

Through the passionate efforts of a great many people in the NeuroNurture community of practice, some fabulous resources have been assembled that we hope will go a long way in assisting students and young adults get through their university and college studies, obtain a job and start a career.

There are resources for autistic students and young adults, parents and carers, employers, university staff, aspiring entrepreneurs, architects & building designers, and senior secondary school students.

For Universities

These resources are designed to help universities become more inclusive and supportive of neurodiverse students. They focus on enhancing the transition from school to university, providing guidance for academic success through coaching and mentoring programs, and offering life skills training. Additionally, these resources aim to improve employment outcomes by facilitating more inclusive career fairs, ensuring that neurodiverse students receive the support they need to thrive both academically and professionally.

For Secondary School Students

These resources are aimed at senior secondary school students to support your exploration and transition into higher education. They provide valuable guidance on navigating the process of choosing and applying to universities, as well as developing essential life skills that will aid in your academic and personal growth.

For School Teachers

These resources are designed to support school teachers in effectively working with students who are on the spectrum or neurodivergent. They offer practical strategies and insights to enhance your understanding and approach, helping you create an inclusive and supportive classroom environment that meets the diverse needs of all students.

Mental Health

This collection of resources aims to foster greater awareness and understanding of mental health challenges within university and workplace settings. It includes materials specifically tailored to support autistic individuals, promoting a more inclusive and supportive environment for all.

For Primary School Students

These resources are tailored to support neurodivergent primary school students, providing tools and strategies to enhance their educational experience. They offer practical guidance on creating a supportive learning environment that accommodates various neurodiverse needs, from sensory-friendly classroom setups to individualized learning approaches. The resources include engaging activities and teaching methods designed to foster inclusion and build on each student's unique strengths. Additionally, they provide strategies for developing essential social and emotional skills, helping students navigate their school experience with confidence and support.

For Parents

This website is designed to assist parents in evaluating their neurodivergent child's academic performance and developmental progress. It offers tools and resources to help parents track their child's growth, identify areas of strength and need, and collaborate with educators to support their learning journey. With a focus on providing actionable insights and fostering a deeper understanding of their child's unique needs, the website empowers parents to advocate effectively and ensure their child receives the support they need to thrive.
